About Echoes:
Echoes is an inclusive communication platform designed to help people who are deaf and mute connect with the world. Our app offers voice-to-text conversion, real-time conversations, and accessibility-focused features so everyone can enjoy music, chat, and interact naturally.

Key Features:
  • Instant voice-to-text conversion for easy communication
  • Support for both individual and group chatting
  • Playlist generation and music streaming for everyone
  • Accessible, user-friendly design and architecture
  • Made for inclusion, happiness, and support
Thank you for joining Echoes. Let's make the world more connected together!
